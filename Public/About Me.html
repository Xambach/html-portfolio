About Me

Welcome to my Portfolio. This is the story of my journey which documents
the challenges I have encountered while learning to become a full-stack developer.
A bit of background: I studied Graphic Design in University - twice, after which
I went into private practice for a good number of years. Although I found this fun
as I was doing what I had always loved to do, I had a lot of limitations. These ranged
from lacking the aptitude to upscale in other skills such as coding and Motion Graphics to 3D and Industrial Design.

Recently however, I decided to face my fears and therefore took up the challenge to learn Maya. I found it wasn't as hard as I'd feared, probably because I immediately
fell in love with hard surface modeling once I waded through online portfolios of
of incredible work by talented artists, some of whom had a similar background to mine.
